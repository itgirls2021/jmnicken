Jayla Nickens' Biography

This continued in her High School years by joining the school’s Art Club and showcasing academic success. She has taken her first steps to do so, by attending Syracuse University Pre College- Online course, IT Girls, so she can get a glimpse of data analytics to prepare herself for the technological aspect of psychology, discover different technology fields, and develop new skills.
